Who We Are

The Official Math Club of Your School

As a student-run club, we provide math training, host competitions, and publish academic materials for high schoolers. We explore mathematics beyond the classroom and build a community that loves problem-solving.

Our Mission

Math Club exists to create a welcoming space where students can explore mathematics beyond the classroom. We compete in regional and national contests, run study sessions, and host events that make math fun and accessible for everyone.

What We Do

  • Competitions — AMC, Mathcounts, and other contests
  • Study Sessions — Weekly problem-solving and concept review
  • Workshops — Special topics and guest speakers
  • Community — A supportive group for anyone who likes math

We meet every Tuesday from 3:30 PM – 4:30 PM in Room 204. No experience required — just bring curiosity and a willingness to learn.

Contact

Questions? Reach out at mathclub@yourschool.edu or find us on social media. We’d love to hear from you.

Join Us

Whether you’re a math olympiad veteran or just getting started, there’s a place for you. Come to any meeting — we’re excited to meet you.
